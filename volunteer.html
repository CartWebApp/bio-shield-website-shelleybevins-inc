Frequently Asked Questions

How much time do I need to commit?
Most volunteer roles require 3-10 hours per month, but we have flexible opportunities that can work with any schedule.
Do I need prior experience?
No prior experience is required! We provide training and support for all volunteer roles. Your enthusiasm and commitment matter most.
Can I volunteer remotely?
Yes! Many of our opportunities can be done remotely, including digital marketing, research, and administrative support.
What support will I receive?
All volunteers receive orientation, ongoing training, regular check-ins with coordinators, and access to our volunteer resources.
